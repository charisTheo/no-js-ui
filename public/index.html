Hello.

I'm a popup element that is open. You can close me by clicking anywhere else in the page. I am also styled in CSS, and can accept arbitrary content. I will remain fixed to the bottom of the window as you scroll the page.

I also have a ::backdrop you can style, which lives between me and the page.
